About me

For Nathan Adcock, the journey from nonprofit leadership to senior software development has been nothing short of transformative. "I’ve always been driven by the desire to make a difference," Nathan shares, reflecting on his 12 years of management experience in the social services sector. Now, with over four years in software development, he’s channeling that same passion into technology. Currently, a Software Developer Level 3 at a leading 340B healthcare solutions company, Nathan has become a key contributor to innovative projects, including secure data masking tools and disaster recovery utilities. With a deep commitment to solving complex problems, Nathan is driven by a singular vision: leveraging technology to create impactful, scalable solutions. He is now setting his sights on advanced roles in architecture and infrastructure, with the ultimate goal of becoming an AI Systems Architect by 2029.

Codeup, CaptureRx, then ...

When asked, Nathan had this to say, "The instructors were excellent, and I gained experience in: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, Java, Spring, and MySQL to name a few". He Graduated in July 2021 and received a position as a web application developer just around the corner from code up at CaptureRx a month after. It has been almost 4 years since then and and Nathan has gained skills in all aspects of Cumulus (CaptureRx's flagship hospital software) as well as in managing the cloud server architechure on which it runs.
