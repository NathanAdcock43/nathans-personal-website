Finding a New Home in Tech

About me

After spending the last ten years working for various social services organizations Nathan Adcock is making a new home in tech. When asked what was the motivation for the change, the San Antonio resident responded, "As much as I loved the work that I was doing before, I have always wanted to develop a skill set that allowed me to problem solve and to create something that people can use".
